How It Works

What is Recallify?

Recallify was created to solve the "inbox clutter" problem. We receive dozens of emails with appointment confirmations, project deadlines, and meetups, only to let them get buried.

With Recallify, you get a custom email inbox. Simply forward emails or schedule notes directly. Our engine automatically parses dates, summaries, and actions, inserting them into your calendar agenda seamlessly.
