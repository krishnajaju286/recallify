Legal & Agreements

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Recallify. By accessing our dashboard or connecting email integrations, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions of service.

1. Service Subscription Tiers
  • Self Managed (Free Tier): Restricted to a maximum of ten (10) active reminders. Natural Language Processing (NLP) email reminder extraction is subject to standard processing speed.
  • Fully Assisted (Premium Tier): Billed annually at ₹649/year (calculated monthly at ₹54/month). Subscribers receive unlimited active reminders, priority NLP contextual parsing, active AI chatbot channels, Google Calendar synchronizations, and direct alerts sent on WhatsApp.
2. Account Activity & Suspension

We log security data including registration metadata, user email configurations, and the customer's last login IP address. The system logs user activity logs to detect loopback violations. The administration reserves the right to suspend or suspend user accounts temporarily or permanently for violations of scheduled email request protocols.

3. Billing, Downgrades, & Cancellation

Premium memberships are billed on a recurring annual schedule of ₹649. Downgrading manually from the Fully Assisted plan back to Self Managed will take effect immediately. Access limits of 10 active reminders will enforce restrictions upon downgrading. Past payments are non-refundable.

Last Updated: July 2026

Recallify is committed to preserving the privacy of our scheduling users. This privacy policy describes the scope of metadata collected, logged, and parsed on the Recallify servers.

1. Information We Collect
  • Identity Data: Username, profile phone numbers, registered email addresses, and date joined metadata.
  • Device Log Data: We automatically record security access logging details including your last login IP address.
  • Activity Data: We monitor your connection activity status (active online indicator dot) by logging your last active request datetime stamp to power the real-time idle page updates.
  • Inbound Email Content: Inbound emails forwarded to our integration inbox are parsed using natural language models to extract event names, times, location forecasts, and checklist details. Raw email bodies are kept strictly inside private user tables and are not shared.
2. Processing & NLP Models

Your emails are processed locally using zero-leak contextual parsing pipelines. Our weather forecasting seed algorithms are deterministic and do not share your coordinates or location logs with public weather networks.

3. Security & Admin Oversight

Registered administrators have access to user profile directory logs to upgrade plans or disable abusive accounts. Card details processed through the secure checkout portal are fully tokenized and stored only in masked format (e.g. Visa ending in 4111).
